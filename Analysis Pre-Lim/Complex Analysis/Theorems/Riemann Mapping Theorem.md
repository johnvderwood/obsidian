Let $\Omega$ be a simply connected proper open subset of $\mathbb{C}$. If $z_0\in \Omega$, then there exists a unique conformal mapping $F:\Omega \to\mathbb{D}$ such that:
	1. $F(z_0)=0$
	2. $F'(z_0)>0$ (forces value to be positive and real)
