Let $f:\mathbb{D} \rightarrow \mathbb{D}$ be holomorphic with $f(0)=0$. Then
	1. $|f(z)|\leq z$ for all $z\in\mathbb{D}$.
	2. $|f'(0)|\leq 1$. 
	3. If either of 1. or 2. have equality, then $f$ is a rotation. i. e., $f(z)=ze^{i\theta}$.
